Who we are

Just4Change is a non-profit organization dedicated to letting other non-profit organizations concentrate on their world changing activities rather than worrying about their fundraising needs. We provide our partners with easy to use fundraising solutions that maximize returns and facilitate outreach to the public.

How it works

Send us an email letting us know which solution(s) you would like to undergo or with any questions you might have.

We'll get back in touch to better customize your fundraiser and send your fundraising pack.

We set up a meeting to get you you're inventory, take pictures or train you on content creation.

You're fundraiser starts a couple of weeks after the initial meeting and we help you every step of the way.

Strip4Change
What it's about

A Strip4Change campaign consists of a team of volunteers or an organization where participants undergo a photoshoot in which they progressively strip. (with everything PG rated of course.) Pictures are then posted on social media sites and donations are solicited. For every 100$ raised a new picture is posted.

Who should participate>

Strip4Change participants have an extensive social network and a large entourage that supports them in reaching their goals. They are willing to do anything in for their cause and expose themselves to expose the problems they want to solve.

Who donates?

People who donate to Strip4Change are generally part of the participant's entourage mainly, their family and friends. What is unique about this fundraiser is that it tends to elicit donations from the young as well as the old.

Fashion4Change
What its about

Fashion4Change is an ongoing campaign where Just4Change sells Fashion accessories to consumers at large. As a Just4Change member you automatically get selected to participate in this campaign. Customers choose which non-profit receives the funds for the sale.

Likes4Change
What its about

A Likes4Change campaign is an adverstizing campaign for large sponsors who would like to donate to a certain cause. Just4Chnage matches these sponsors with their sponsored non-profits and helps in the creation of a video for which sponsors pay for every like or share on a social media site.

FAQ
Where does the money go?

Just4Change keeps a 20% retainer on donations to cover our costs. We forward the rest of the money directly to the charity of your choice.

What kind of donors do you target?

We attract donors from all age groups. Our fundraisers have a tendency to attract a much younger crowd than traditional fundraisers. Though this population of donors isn't the most affluent it tends to be one that is extremely conciouss of different social issues as well as being a population that gets the word around.

Do I need to be a registered charity?

No. In your application for membership you will be asked to provide proof that you are a registered non-profit organization accorfing to the governement of canada. If you do hold charity status make sure to note it on your application so that we can workout tax exemption receipts.

Do you do custom fundraisers?

All our fundraising solutions have a unique component where the solutions are tailored to a particular cause or organization. However we currently do not host custom events. If you have an idea for a new fundraising solution let us know at J4C.contact@gmail.com.
